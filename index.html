Hi, I’m Tim Ezekiel, an eCommerce specialist based in Whistler, Canada. With over seven years of experience in the digital space, I’ve dedicated my career to helping businesses establish a strong online presence and achieve remarkable results.

My expertise covers a wide range of eCommerce services, from designing and developing responsive, user-friendly websites to setting up digital products and marketplaces on platforms like Shopify, Wix, Squarespace, WordPress, Etsy, and Amazon. I also specialize in technical and on-page optimization, SEO, website monetization for eCommerce blogs, and creating strategies that drive sustainable growth.

Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working with more than 150 eCommerce store owners, helping them build stores that generate seven-figure monthly revenue. I focus on dropshipping, print-on-demand, and digital stores in niches such as fashion, clothing, cosmetics, and electronics.

My approach emphasizes creating websites with exceptional user experiences, optimizing them for search engines, and implementing marketing strategies that drive sales and foster long-term success.

My Services

eCommerce Website Design and Development

I design and develop responsive, user-friendly eCommerce websites on platforms like Shopify, Wix, Squarespace, and WordPress. My focus is on creating visually appealing, high-converting stores that enhance customer experience and drive sales.

Digital Product Design and Marketplace Setup

I design and set up digital products such as planners, templates, and printables tailored to your target audience. Additionally, I help establish marketplaces on platforms like Etsy and Amazon, ensuring your digital products are presented to maximize their appeal and sales potential.

SEO and Optimization Services

I provide technical and on-page SEO services to improve website visibility and performance. This includes optimizing product pages, blog content, and site structure to rank higher on search engines and attract organic traffic.

Marketing Strategies for Sustainable Growth
